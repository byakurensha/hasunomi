# Meeting Minutes

1. Ensure project-work is active and preserve accepted source in `Materials/`.
2. Do not invent decisions, owners, deadlines, or attendance.
3. Structure participants, agenda, discussion, decisions, open questions, and
   next actions.
4. When design intent is unclear, ask whether to use the supplied standard A4
   template or a free design.
5. The standard path uses the same A4 renderer, not a second design template.
   Map major agenda or decision groups to heading level 1, agenda items to
   level 2, and subordinate items to level 3. It creates one editable ODT by
   default; PDF is explicit only.
6. Verify facts, visible table borders, cell padding, and unresolved statements
   before delivery. Also verify that Japanese title and heading text is visibly
   bold and that short label columns do not consume half of a two-column table.
