# Meeting Minutes

Follow the template choice gate in the core rules before creating files.

1. Ensure project-work is active and preserve accepted source in `Materials/`.
2. Do not invent decisions, owners, deadlines, or attendance.
3. Use participants, agenda, discussion, decisions, open questions, and next
   actions only when the meeting material makes them useful. They are examples,
   not a mandatory form. A decision meeting, an idea session, and a status
   meeting can use different component selections and orders.
4. The standard path uses the same A4 renderer, not a second design template.
   Use the shared title, optional lead, headings, tables, callouts, note areas,
   and footer as needed. Map major groups to heading level 1, agenda items to
   level 2, and subordinate items to level 3. It creates one editable ODT by
   default; PDF is explicit only.
5. Verify facts, visible table borders, cell padding, and unresolved statements
   before delivery. Also verify that Japanese title and heading text is visibly
   bold and that short label columns do not consume half of a two-column table.
